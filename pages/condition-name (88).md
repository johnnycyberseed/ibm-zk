- why "88"?
	- The [[CODASYL]] team design level numbers in decades:
		- 01..49 :: structure
		- 66 :: renames
		- 77 :: stand-alone items
	- at that time in the design process, "88" was the next available slot
		- it also makes it easy to spot in a listing
